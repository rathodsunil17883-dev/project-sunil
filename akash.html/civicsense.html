Keep Your City Clean

Dispose of garbage properly and avoid littering on streets, parks, and public spaces.

Report Street Issues

Notify authorities about broken streetlights, potholes, and damaged public facilities.

Save Water

Use water responsibly, fix leaks, and encourage others to conserve water.

Follow Traffic Rules

Obey traffic signals, wear helmets, and encourage safe driving habits.

Plant Trees

Participate in tree planting drives to make your surroundings green and healthy.

Help Your Community

Assist neighbors, elderly, and contribute to a cooperative and friendly environment.

Save Electricity

Switch off unused lights and appliances to save energy and reduce pollution.

Use Public Transport

Reduce traffic congestion and pollution by using buses, trains, or shared vehicles.

Recycle Waste

Separate recyclable and non-recyclable waste and encourage others to recycle.

Respect Public Property

Do not damage benches, walls, or public facilities; treat them as your own.

Follow Sanitation Rules

Use public toilets responsibly and maintain hygiene in community areas.
